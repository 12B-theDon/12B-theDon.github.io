About

Minhyeong Lee

I am an undergraduate student in Smart Mobility Engineering at Inha University, with experience in building robotic systems, developing SLAM-based autonomous driving pipelines, and implementing imitation and reinforcement learning in MuJoCo. Based on these experiences, I am interested in general robot policies and Vision-Language-Action models, particularly in bridging the gap between simulated training and reliable real-world deployment through world-model-based policy learning and robust robot control for complex human-centered environments.
